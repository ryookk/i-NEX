About



i-NEX PHILIPPINES CORPORATION, is a registered corporation duly organized and engaged in the bussiness of importation and distributions of i-NEX medical, laboratory consumable products and medical devices.
I-NEX is also a part owner of NEXCO MEDICAL ENTERPRISES, a company that diligently deals and makes expertly for medical distribution directly to the consumers and end users since 2012.
Upholding the idea of promoting medical care, we have been making great efforts to continuously provide safe and effective medical products in industry base on our strengths and efforts.
Together with partnership of some of the manufacturer abroad, I-NEX able to assess and choose the quality products that may satisfied and guaranteed competitive price offer to the customers.
